I am a Brazilian photographer and digital designer based in Dublin, Ireland. My work focuses on portrait, fashion and contemporary photography, with an interest in people, stories and visual atmosphere.

With a background in journalism, photography and digital design, I create images that combine careful composition, emotion and a strong sense of presence.
